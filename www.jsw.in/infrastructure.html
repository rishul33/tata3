JSW Infrastructure is part of the diversified US$ 13 billion JSW Group. As one of India’s leading business houses, JSW Group also has other business interests in sectors such as steel, energy, cement, paints, sports and venture capital. JSW Infrastructure is ranked among the Top 5 Port companies in India and operates environment-friendly seaports & terminals. Its seven state-of the-art port facilities flanked across East, West & Southern regions of the country are a testament to its commitment to nation-building with best-in-class infrastructure. These facilities are designed to make JSW Infrastructure a benchmark for efficiency and sustainability in port operations. Its international presence includes a terminal it operates at Fujairah, UAE.  The existing ports & terminals of the Company are capable of handling a wide range of cargo and vessels up to Cape size. Its fully mechanised state-of-the-art facilities enable faster turnaround of vessels while ensuring efficient use of existing resources and logistical advantage to its customers. JSW Infrastructure is continuously expanding its cargo-handling facilities and has healthy mix of captive and external cargo business. As part of its future growth strategy, the Company plans to enhance its overall cargo-handling capacity to 200 MTPA within the next few years. It is also strengthening its market position through a holistic approach by focusing on value-added offerings with end-to-end logistic support and a decent cargo profile. The company has been acknowledged for its business excellence from time to time. JSW Infrastructure is committed to strengthening its ESG performance across the operational ecosystem by aligning its policies and practices with international standards.
