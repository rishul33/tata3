From exposure of natural work environments to learning with people who excel at their work, JSW is always looking for innovative, determined and ambitious youngsters.

Every year through JSW Campus Connect initiative, the recruitment team handpicks the finest talents. The selected candidates are assigned live projects with corresponding business units. The mentors hone their skill sets and a Management panel evaluates each project at the end of completion basis which growth path is defined.
