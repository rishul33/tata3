Initiatives for a sustainable tomorrow.

Since its inception, the JSW foundation has taken conscious steps to support and sustain communities located around their plants. With increasing importance placed on sustainable development, we have spearheaded programmes that contribute to a stable tomorrow.

The Mid-day Meal programme, the Tamanna School for specially-abled children and the Sanjeevani Hospital, have been our way of giving back.
