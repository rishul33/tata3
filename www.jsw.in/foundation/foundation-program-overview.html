Nurturing the Nation

At JSW Foundation, we aim to offer every Indian a chance to build a better life

AT A GLANCE

JSW Foundation is committed to creating more smiles at every step of the journey. The organisation is in constant pursuit of making life better for communities with its various initiatives in the fields of health, education, livelihood and sports along with art and culture.

JSW’s effluent plants, green townships and happy employees are changing the course of the nation with their spirited growth. The company is proud to be charting a course to excellence that creates opportunities for every Indian and leads to the creation of a dynamic and developed nation.
