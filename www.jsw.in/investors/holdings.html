JSW Holdings
JSW Holdings is a Non-Banking Financial Company (NBFC) that forms the investment arm of the JSW Group.

The company’s main holdings are in the Group’s Steel companies.

With the steel sector on the growth path, JSW Holdings is on a sound financial footing.

The company will continue to focus on strategic investments in new ventures promoted by the JSW Group.
