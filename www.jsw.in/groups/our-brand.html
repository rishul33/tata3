BETTER EVERYDAY

Bettering ourselves helps us make ‘better’ happen for everyone else. We help ‘better’ happen for all the lives we touch – that means our employees, our business associates, our customers and the communities around the plants and facilities and the industry fraternity at large.

Brand Personality

 

WE ARE DRIVEN BY GROWTH.

We pursue growth zealously. In the face of all challenges, the most effective way to overcome them was to grow. Faster than the market, bigger than the competition.

WE TAKE BIG BUT CALIBRATED RISKS.

Big rewards never came without the big risks. But not reckless ones. We had the remarkable ability to take measured but worthwhile risks, to go down uncharted territory knowing very well what could go wrong – and being prepared for it!

WE BELIEVE IN GIVING BACK.

Our commitment to the nation goes beyond infrastructure or heritage - it is equally about the environment and society that provided us with the opportunities to grow.

WE’RE EFFICIENT.

Dreaming big and overcoming challenges with limited resources helps us push ourselves. Perhaps, this is what helped us hone our acclaimed operational efficiency and our financial acumen.

WE’RE FIERCELY NATIONALISTIC.

We have and always will be committed to the betterment of our people, our society and our nation. This spirit is reflected in the projects we undertake, the heritage that we strive to preserve and the societies we support.

Business Standard Award 2018

JSW Group’s Chairman and MD, Mr. Sajjan Jindal, was honoured with the ‘Business Standard CEO of the Year’ award, in recognition of his indomitable spirit and progressive work in the steel industry.

He was presented the award by the Defence Minister of India, Nirmala Sitharaman, who also acknowledged the company’s excellent initiatives in the CSR space.

India Business Leaders Award 2019

Mr. Sajjan Jindal, Chairman and MD of JSW Group, was recently felicitated with the ‘Outstanding Business Leader of the Year Award’ at the 14th edition of the CNBC IBLA Awards held in Mumbai.

The event was conducted to honour leaders across industries who have made revolutionary advancements in their sphere and helmed a wave of change in the country.
