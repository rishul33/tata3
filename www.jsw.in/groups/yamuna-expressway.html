Yamuna Expressway

Overview

The Yamuna Expressway, inaugurated in 2014, is India's longest motorway at 165 kms.

Featured in the World's Top 100 Innovative Infrastructure Projects by KPMG, this 6-lane expressway drastically reduced the travel time between Delhi and Agra. And it's a beautiful drive at that. JSW was proud to be part of a project that has gone on to become a milestone in road and transport development. We not only ensured a timely delivery, we were also successful in meeting international standards, in building an expressway that can withstand the test of time.
